## Part 2: Before/After Content Guidelines

### Photo Requirements

**Technical Standards:**
| Aspect | Requirement |
|--------|-------------|
| Resolution | Minimum 1080x1080 for social |
| Lighting | Same lighting in both photos (overhead or natural) |
| Angle | Same angle (front, crown, side) |
| Distance | Same distance from camera |
| Background | Neutral preferred, same in both |
| Format | JPEG/PNG, high quality |

**Timing Guidelines:**
- **Minimum duration:** 6 months between photos
- **Ideal duration:** 12 months
- **Update intervals:** 3-month progress shots for threads

---

### Photo Angle Guide

**1. Crown View (Top-Down)**
- Camera directly above head
- Look straight down at floor
- Best for crown thinning
- Include reference point (mole, etc.)

**2. Hairline View (Frontal)**
- Camera at eye level
- Pull hair back fully
- Forehead visible
- Neutral expression
- Best for receding hairline

**3. Temple View (Side)**
- 45-degree angle
- Show temple region
- Natural hair position
- Best for temple recession

**4. Diffuse Thinning (Part Line)**
- Part hair in same spot
- Camera slightly above
- Best for overall thinning
- Include scalp visibility reference

---

---

### Editing Guidelines

**Allowed:**
- Cropping for composition
- Brightness/contrast adjustment (SAME for both photos)
- Color correction (SAME for both photos)
- Background blur/removal (if noted)
- Adding text overlays

**NOT Allowed:**
- Changing hair appearance (density, color, coverage)
- Using different lighting adjustments between photos
- AI enhancement of hair
- Misleading time stamps
- Removing/hiding visible scalp areas

## Part 4: Content Collection System

### How to Collect User Stories

**Step 1: Request Form**
Create Google Form/Typeform with fields:
- Name (first only for public use)
- Email
- Which trial/product are you using?
- How long have you been using it?
- Describe your experience in 2-3 sentences
- Would you share before/after photos? (Y/N)
- Can we feature your story? (Consent checkbox)

**Step 2: Photo Collection**
- Request photos via secure upload (not email)
- Require photos of same angle/lighting
- Include date stamps
- Get signed consent form

**Step 3: Verification**
- Confirm user is actual customer/trial participant
- Cross-reference with internal records
- Flag any inconsistencies

**Step 4: Content Creation**
- Write draft using templates above
- Send to user for approval
- Get final sign-off before posting
- Schedule across platforms
