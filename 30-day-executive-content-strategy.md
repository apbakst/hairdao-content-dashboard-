## üî• Content Templates

### 1. The Friday Night Series (@HairDAO_)
Format: "It's Friday night. [Hair-related question/observation]? [Advice/wisdom] üîë"
Example: "It's Friday night. Do you have your hair? Stay prepared, young padewan üîë"
Why it works: Ritualistic, relatable, memeable

### 2. The Hot Take Thread (@andrew_bakst)
Format: "[Controversial statement]. Here's why: [5-7 tweet thread with evidence]"
Example: "The FDA approval process kills more people than it saves. Here's why:"
Why it works: Engagement bait + education

### 3. The Science Explainer (@VerbinnenAndrew)
Format: "Most people don't understand [concept]. Let me explain: [simple breakdown]"
Example: "Most people don't understand DHT. Let me explain in 60 seconds."
Why it works: Positions as expert, provides value

### 4. The Behind-the-Scenes (@AJTraxler)
Format: "[What we're building/doing] + [real photo/screenshot]"
Example: "What building a DeSci company actually looks like: [messy whiteboard photo]"
Why it works: Authenticity, shows real work

### 5. The Promo Drop (@HairDAO_)
Format: "[Value prop]. Use code [CODE] for [discount]. [Link]"
Example: "40% off all Hair Growth treatments. Code: 40OFFHAIR. Link in bio."
Why it works: Clear CTA, drives massive reach (231K+ views)
