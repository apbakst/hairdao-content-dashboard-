## ğŸ¨ Design Principles

### Typography
- **Impact font** for classic memes
- **Bold sans-serif** for modern memes
- High contrast (white text, black stroke)
- Readable at thumbnail size

### Colors
- Use HairDAO emerald (#10b981) for branding
- Dark backgrounds perform better on Twitter
- High contrast = more engagement

### Composition
- Text should be instantly readable
- Don't overcrowd
- Leave space for the joke to breathe
