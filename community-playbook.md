## 1. Twitter Engagement Strategy

### Daily Routine (30 min total)

**Morning Session (15 min) - 8-9 AM**
1. Check notifications & reply to all mentions (5 min)
2. Engage with 3-5 relevant posts from target accounts (5 min)
3. Quote tweet or reply to 1 industry news item (5 min)

**Evening Session (15 min) - 6-7 PM**
1. Reply to afternoon mentions (5 min)
2. Engage with community posts about hair loss/DeSci (5 min)
3. Post or schedule next day's content (5 min)

### Who to Engage With

**Priority Tier (Daily)**
- Hair loss researchers & scientists
- DeSci/biotech projects (VitaDAO, Molecule, etc.)
- Longevity & biohacking influencers
- People sharing hair loss journeys
- $HAIR token holders & community members

**Secondary Tier (2-3x weekly)**
- Crypto/Web3 media outlets
- Health & wellness influencers
- Dermatologists on Twitter
- Biotech VCs and analysts

**Opportunity Tier (As they appear)**
- Celebrities/influencers discussing hair loss
- Viral hair loss content
- Breaking research news

### Identifying & Nurturing Superfans

**Signs of a Superfan:**
- Responds to most announcements
- Helps newcomers without being asked
- Creates content (memes, threads, videos)
- Defends the project authentically
- Shares ideas and feedback regularly

**How to Nurture Them:**
1. **Recognize publicly** - "Shoutout to @user for always helping newcomers!"
2. **Engage privately** - DM to thank them, ask for feedback
3. **Give early access** - Share news before public announcements
4. **Create roles** - Community Mod, Research Champion, Meme Lord
5. **Include in decisions** - "What do you think about X?"

**Superfan Outreach DM:**
```
Hey [name]! 

I've noticed you're super active and helpful in the Discord. Really appreciate you being part of this community.

Wanted to reach out directly‚Äîwould love to hear any feedback you have on how we're doing or ideas for the community. You clearly get what we're building.

Also, if you're interested in taking on a more official role (like helping moderate or contributing to content), let me know. No pressure at all, just wanted to put it out there.

Thanks for being here üôè
```

### Moderation Guidelines

**Immediate Ban:**
- Scam links/phishing
- Doxxing
- Harassment/threats
- Spam bots

**Warning then Ban:**
- Repeated off-topic promotion
- Toxic behavior after warning
- Spreading FUD without engaging

**Gentle Redirect:**
- Off-topic conversations ‚Üí "Love the energy! Let's move this to #general"
- Heated debates ‚Üí "Great points on both sides. Let's keep it constructive"
- Basic questions ‚Üí "Good question! Check out #faq, and ask in #help-desk if you need more"

**Mod Response Template (for rule violations):**
```
Hey [name], just a heads up that [specific behavior] isn't quite the vibe we're going for here. [Brief explanation of why]. 

No hard feelings‚Äîlet's keep the community positive for everyone. Thanks for understanding! üôè
```

### When to Engage vs. Ignore

**ENGAGE when:**
| Situation | Why |
|-----------|-----|
| Legitimate question from real account | Builds trust, helps others watching |
| Influencer or large account | High visibility, worth addressing |
| Factual inaccuracies spreading | Correct the record before it spreads |
| Someone who seems genuinely confused | Easy win, they might become supporters |

**IGNORE when:**
| Situation | Why |
|-----------|-----|
| Obvious troll with tiny following | Don't amplify, waste of time |
| Repeat offender who won't change mind | Diminishing returns |
| Clearly bad faith actor | Engaging validates them |
| Late-night rage post | Often deletes itself, let it go |

**THE 24-HOUR RULE:**
For non-urgent criticism, wait 24 hours before responding. Often:
- The post gets no traction
- Others defend you first
- You craft a better response with cooler head

| Medium | Influencer criticism | 4-6 hours | Senior team |
| High | Viral negative thread | 1-2 hours | Core team + legal review |
| Critical | Security incident, major FUD | Immediate | All hands, coordinated response |
